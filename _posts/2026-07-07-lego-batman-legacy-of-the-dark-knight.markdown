I finally completed [LEGO Batman: Legacy of the Dark Knight](https://www.metacritic.com/game/lego-batman-legacy-of-the-dark-knight) on PlayStation 5 yesterday and obtained the all-important Platinum trophy. Everything from the graphics to the music to the voice acting was top notch. The open-world Gotham was particularly impressive. I really enjoyed my time with the game and highly recommend it.
