I recently finished watching the fourth season of [The Lincoln Lawyer](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt13833978/) on Netflix and, just like the previous three seasons, really enjoyed it. The whole cast is great, and the story, based on Michael Connelly's excellent book The Law of Innocence, was gripping even though I already knew the ending. I can't wait for the fifth season!
