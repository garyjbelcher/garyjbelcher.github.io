My beloved [Aston Villa](https://www.avfc.co.uk/) are just one game away from their first major European trophy in 44 years after [thrashing Nottingham Forest in the semi-final of the Europa League last night](https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/live/c0m2v0zzkgyt). The disappointment of losing the semi-final of the Europa Conference League to Olympiakos two years ago was well and truly banished in spectacular style. Can they go all the way in Istanbul on May 20th? I sure hope so!
