My beloved [Aston Villa](https://www.avfc.co.uk/) won the Europa League on Wednesday evening, [beating SC Freiburg 3-0 in Istanbul](https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/live/c893dkqjv00t) to win their first trophy in 30 years and their first major European trophy in 44 years. I couldn't be there for the final, but I was there for the epic victory parade on Thursday afternoon. Hopefully, this is just the start of many more trophies to come!
