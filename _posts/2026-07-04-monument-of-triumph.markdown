I've been playing the last ever content update to Destiny 2, Monument of Triumph, on and off over the past month or so. I'd be playing a lot more, though, if I wasn't watching a lot of World Cup games - and that is because the update is so good. It is exactly what the game needed, and it absolutely boggles the mind as to why Bungie never did this sooner.
