I read an article yesterday [about cinema etiquette](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/c2482ql0zp4o). It asks, "Is behavior getting worse?" In my view, it is: so much so that I cancelled my Cineworld Unlimited membership last August and haven't been back since. I'd much rather wait and watch a movie in the comfort of my own home on one of the many streaming services. Why pay all that money only for it to be spoiled by people talking and blinding you with their mobile phone screens?
