I completed [Cat Quest 2](https://www.metacritic.com/game/cat-quest-ii-the-lupus-empire/) over the weekend. I loved the first game, so I was eager to play the sequel just as soon as I could fit it in. And it did not disappoint. It is very much more of the same, albeit slightly bigger than the first game, but that's not a bad thing. I thoroughly enjoyed my time in the game, and I can't wait to play Cat Quest 3!
